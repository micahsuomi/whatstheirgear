From Musicians, For Musicians

Our app is designed from musicians for musicians. It lets you track your favorite artists' gear on any song you listen.

Do you want to know what guitar, amp or pedals does John Mayer use on Gravity? Fear no more. Our app connects directly with Spotify and direct you to the artist's gear of choice for a specific song.
